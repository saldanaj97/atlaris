## Outcome

By the end of April 14, the prelim refactor work had moved from recommendations into real structure:

- env/config ownership was split into facets,
- error and formatting utilities had one shared home,
- app/feature boundaries were enforced,
- and the plan read side had a stable canonical seam for later lifecycle work.

The common theme was front-loading the ownership cleanup so the higher-risk generation slices could land on clearer foundations.
